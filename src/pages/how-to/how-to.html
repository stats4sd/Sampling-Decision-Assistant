How to Use the guide

This page will include a video screencast demonstrating how to use the tool, including:

  • Using the main step-by-step assistant
  • Adding answers and information to the questions presented, and getting a written report out at the end
  • Referencing the training and extra information presented throughout the step-by-step process

The how-to guide will also be written as a brief text document for people unable to easily access the video, or who prefer to refrence a written document to find help
